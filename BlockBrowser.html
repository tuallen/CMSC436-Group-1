Block Browser is an app that allows users to search their neighborhood to view and create ratings for where they live. Our app uses the AARP livability categories (housing, neighborhood, transportation, environment, health, engagement, and opportunity) and resident reviews to display information on the community. On the neighborhood hub page, a user can view the map of the community, the average score based on user ratings, and resident reviews.
